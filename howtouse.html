How to Use the DnD Dice Roller

Getting Started

Welcome to the DnD Dice Roller! This tool is designed to help you roll dice for Dungeons & Dragons and other tabletop games. Here's how to get started:

  1. Select the number of dice you want to roll.
  2. Choose the type of dice (d2, d3, d4, etc.).
  3. Optionally, add a modifier to each roll or to the total.
  4. Click the "Roll" button to see your results.
  5. Your roll history will appear in the log on the right side of the screen.

Adding and Removing Dice

You can add multiple dice sets by clicking the "+" button next to the dice roller. To remove a dice set, click the "-" button on the corresponding line.

Saving and Loading Rolls

If you want to save your dice settings for your next session, simply enable the save option by clicking the disk icon. Your settings will be stored locally in your browser.

Exporting the Log

You can save your roll history by exporting the log to a PDF file. This is useful for keeping track of your rolls during a gaming session.
